Our Story

Three Decades of Excellence

Since 1993, Karate Budokan Federation International has stood as a beacon of martial arts excellence, preserving the authentic traditions of Okinawan Karate while nurturing champions who have represented our federation on the world stage.

Our dojo has produced over 150 black belts, 50+ international champions, and trained more than 3,000 students across 25 countries. We believe Karate is not just a martial art — it's a way of life.

Our Mission

To provide world-class martial arts education that develops physical excellence, mental fortitude, and moral character in every student.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in traditional Karate education, producing champions and leaders who embody the true spirit of Budokan.

Our Philosophy

Mind, Body, Spirit — unified through discipline. Karate begins and ends with respect. The ultimate aim is not victory, but perfection of character.

Our Journey

Historical Timeline

1993

Foundation

Karate Budokan Federation established in Okinawa, Japan by Grand Master Takeshi Yamamoto.

2000

International Expansion

Opened first international branches in Europe and North America.

2010

World Championship Victory

Our athletes won gold at the World Karate Championship for the first time.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched online training platform, reaching students in 50+ countries.

2026

Global Leader

Now operating in 25+ countries with 3,000+ active students worldwide.
