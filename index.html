About Our Academy

The Legacy of Budokan

Founded in 1993, Karate Budokan Federation International has grown into one of the world's most respected martial arts institutions, preserving the ancient traditions of Okinawan Karate while embracing modern training methodologies.

Our Mission

To cultivate discipline, respect, and physical excellence through authentic Karate training.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in traditional martial arts education and championship development.

Our Philosophy

Mind, Body, Spirit — unified through the disciplined practice of the Way of the Empty Hand.
